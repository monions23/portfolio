Gestalt Principles

Project Description

  • Compositions based on the six Gestalt principles
    • Figure/ground, symmetry, similarity, continuation, closure, proximity
  • Software used: Adobe Illustrator
  • Simple shapes such as triangles, circles, and squares
  • Throughout my creative process, I thought about:
    • The arrangement of the shapes
    • How to use positive and negative space
