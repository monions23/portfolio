Tracking Bigfoot Sightings

Project Description

  • Completed as a lab assignment for GEOG:2500: Foundations of GIS
  • Data related to tornado tracks taken from the FEMA Geospatial Resource Center
    • As a shapefile
  • Census data added from ArcGIS Online
  • Created a multitude of spatial and attribute queries based off of the data
    • Largely related to SQL
    • Practiced creating multi-layered selections

What I Learned

  • How to effectively apply different combinations of attribute and spatial queries in ArcGIS
  • How the interface for creating ArcGIS queries relates to writing SQL queries
  • How to analyze a wide variety of different selections within the same map
    • E.g., how the results of different queries can relate to each other
