About Me

My name is Meredith Onions, and I am a junior at the University of Iowa studying informatics with an art cognate alongside a double minor in computer science and sociology. This means that I am obtaining relevant skills in data analysis and computing as well as usability principles and graphic design. I am also deepening my algorithmic knowledge, and I am developing a strong understanding of society and group behavior.

Outside of school, I work as an academic research assistant within a university lab that is primarily focused on human-computer interaction. I also serve as a lead volunteer at the university’s food pantry. I design lights for the university’s theatre department as well, which allows me to effectively combine my creative and technical skills.

I am very interested in careers related to web development and data visualization. I want to innovate and solve problems in the world of technology, and I want to make the world a better place in doing so. I developed this website to present a little more about me and my work.
