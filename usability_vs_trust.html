Survey of Usability vs Trust

Project Description

  • Completed for a project in STATS:1020: Elementary Statistics and Interference
  • Developed a quantitative question to survey respondents with:
    • Think about the website or app which you use the most.
    • On a scale of 1 to 5 (1 being terrible and 5 being amazing), how usable do you find that website/app to be?
    • Think about what data you believe that website/app collects about you. On a scale of 1 to 5 (1 being not at all and 5 being completely), how much do you trust that website/app with this data?
  • Received 17 respondents
  • Copied and plotted the data in Microsoft Excel
  • Analyzed the best fit line and correlation between the two variables

What I Learned

  • How to effectively conduct a qualitative survey with regression analysis in mind
  • How to create a scatter plot and find the best fit line in Microsoft Excel
  • How to find the regression equation of the data and relate that to my general conclusion
